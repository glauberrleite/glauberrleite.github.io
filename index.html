Projects

RAMOS Ink

Converts InkML files received from a Livescribe Smartpen's penlet to image and/or video. RAMOS Ink was build from a research in the Center of Excellence for Social Technologies (NEES - Núcleo de Excelência em Tecnologias Sociais). The application is part of a bigger software called RAMOS, which have a more complete set of features to aid researchers in Brazil to study writing processes in real-time.

Lexicanalytics

Lexicanalytics offers a set of functionalities to help linguistics researchers with lexical studies, when they are manipulating and retrieving information from text data.
